What is this?

This is Team 116's hack for HackTJ 2019! We've created a collection of games over the 24 hours that we have been given to complete the hack, and this is the final result. After certain events in each game, the player is asked some questions about finance to help educate the world in a fun, playful way.

That's Cool!

For more cool things, check out the GitHub at www.github.com/myworldrules/myworldrules.github.io. We've posted the project's code there.

Any feedback is welcome!

Where Do You Learn?

The team concurrently attends John Champe High School or Stone Bridge High School and the Academy of Engineering and Technology at the Academies of Loudoun. At the Academies, we take Information Technology and Computer Science courses.
